How can I get a process update on my mobile/Web development project ?

Getting a status update on your project is very important. As a client, you want to make sure that your projects are being managed and that they’re still on track to be finished when expected. The best way to stay informed about your project is to set regular meetings with your mobile application development team members. During these meetings, have each person explain what their responsibilities are for that day/week/month and what you can expect from them. Meetings like these will ensure that both parties know how far along in the process everyone is and give everyone an idea of what’s coming up next.
